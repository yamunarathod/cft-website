Show Stoppers

Nebula

Nebula is a product that is capable of displaying 3D volumetric content and responding to sensors and generating responses. Here, it is more realistic as the imagery is a 3 dimensional portrayal. Software integrated sensors can be customized as per your requirements coinciding with the themes.

Aurora

Aurora is a 2D LED display technology where the LED strips are arranged in a linear format. This systematic arrangement of lights enables the audience. Our software integration with the product gives way to present content on it. The integrated sensors enable the audience to have an immersive experience with the product. You can integrate stunning visuals, AI integrated responses and even showreels.
